# Suggested Features for Finance Math Lab

Based on the current architecture, here are 10 high-impact features to further enhance the learning experience:

1. **Fixed Income & Bond Lab**
   - Add a module for calculating Bond Prices, Yield to Maturity (YTM), and Duration.
   - Interactive visualization: Show how bond prices move inversely to interest rate changes (the "see-saw" effect).

2. **Mortgage & Loan Amortization Lab**
   - A tool to visualize how interest vs. principal payments shift over a 15 or 30-year loan.
   - Show the dramatic impact of making just one extra payment per year on total interest saved.

3. **Inflation & Purchasing Power Simulator**
   - A "Time Machine" utility that calculates the future cost of a loaf of bread or a degree based on historical inflation rates.
   - Teaches the difference between *nominal* returns and *real* returns.

4. **Retirement & "FIRE" Calculator**
   - A logic-driven lab where students enter target ages and spending habits to see their "Safe Withdrawal Rate" (The 4% Rule).
   - Visualizes the "Nest Egg" required to live off dividends/interest alone.

5. **Real Estate ROI & Cap Rate Lab**
   - Math for calculating Gross Rent Multipliers, Cap Rates, and Cash-on-Cash returns.
   - Teaches the concept of "leverage" (using a loan to amplify returns).

6. **Taxes & Net Drag Module**
   - Simulations showing how Capital Gains taxes (short-term vs. long-term) impact the final value of a compounding portfolio.
   - Highlights the value of tax-advantaged accounts like Roth IRAs.

7. **Crypto & High-Volatility Playground**
   - A comparison lab testing "Steady Blue Chips" vs. "Wild Crypto" to see how massive drawdowns (~90%) require much larger gains (~900%) just to break even.

8. **Alternative Logic: Game Theory & Auction Math**
   - A module on "The Winner's Curse" and basic bidding math used in IPOs or eBay-style auctions.

9. **Credit Score & Interest Rate Nexus**
   - A visual lab showing how a lower credit score leads to higher APRs, and the literal dollar cost of a "Bad Credit" vs. "Excellent Credit" car loan.

10. **ESG & Value-Based Weighted Portfolios**
    - A tool that lets users "exclude" industries (like tobacco or oil) and re-calculate the efficient frontier to see the "cost" or "benefit" of ethical investing.
